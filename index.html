The Set-Up sprint, day by day

Two weeks. No decks. No overrun.

Every Set-Up follows the same shape. You know what’s happening and when.

Day 01 · Monday

Kickoff & audit findings

90-minute kickoff. We confirm the three workflows, the stack they’ll live in, the people they’ll serve. You get the written brief in your inbox that afternoon.

Days 02–04 · Week 1

First workflow live

Usually the one with the biggest day-to-day impact (inbox, meeting prep, or CRM). Built end-to-end in your own tools. You’re using it by Thursday.

Days 05–08 · Week 2

Workflows two & three

Built in parallel. Short daily checkpoints over Loom or Slack — no extra meetings unless we hit a choice point that needs you.

Day 09 · Thursday

Team handover

60-minute walkthrough for anyone on your team who’ll run them. Written docs covering every prompt, API key, integration and fallback.

Day 10 · Friday

Live & handed over

Three workflows running in your stack. You own them. We’re available for two weeks of light support, after which you either upgrade to the Companion or we wrap.

What you’re probably thinking

The honest answers.

What if none of the workflows are worth it?

You get a full refund. That’s the only guarantee anyone in this category can honestly make, so we make it. It also almost never happens — there’s always something.

Don’t I just need ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is great for talking to. It’s not great for running your inbox while you sleep, or updating your CRM from a call transcript. Those need building. That’s what we do.

What tools do you use?

Whatever fits: Claude, GPT, Zapier, Make, n8n, Gmail & Calendar APIs, Attio, Notion, plus custom scripts where helpful. We build in your stack, not a bespoke one.

Is this one person, really?

Yes. Which means bookings are limited. It also means you always deal with the person who built the thing — not an account manager, not a junior, not an agency layer.

Who is this not for?

If you’ve got an engineering team already wiring AI into your product, this isn’t for you — you need a fractional AI CTO. If you’re pre-product and want a “strategy”, also no. This is for founders who want fewer hours spent on the boring stuff.

Can I talk first before paying for the audit?

No. The £750 buys the conversation and the brief. It filters out people who aren’t serious and pays for the thinking. If you don’t want to pay for a conversation about AI, you probably don’t need one.
