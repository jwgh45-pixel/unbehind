The build, by day

One week of evenings.

Assumes we kick off Monday. I work in the evenings around the day job.

Mon · Day 1

Setup

You upgrade LinkedIn to Sales Navigator Advanced (5 min, £80/mo on your account). I build the saved search across your 21 target companies + Swedish + English search terms + Stockholm region.

Tue · Day 2

Personalisation script

I build the AI script that reads a candidate’s LinkedIn profile and drafts your first message. Trained on the ‘Hi, I hope you are well’ message you sent me + the GlobalConnect role context.

Wed · Day 3

First long list

I run the search, export to spreadsheet, push each candidate through the script. Output: 50-80 names with drafts. I quality-check the AI output and kill the duds before you see anything.

Thu · Day 4

Attio + Granola

I build your Attio workspace and import the candidate list. Set up Granola on a quick call (5 min). Record the Loom walkthrough.

Fri · Day 5

Handover

I send you the Loom, the spreadsheet, the Attio link. You sit down, open the list, send the 50-80 messages manually through LinkedIn. Roughly 30 minutes if you’re quick.

Days 7-14

Replies land

You move people through the Attio kanban as they reply. Aim: 15-25% reply rate (vs the ~5-10% generic outreach gets). We jump on a 20-min review call to see what worked.

What you do

Four things. Honestly that’s it.

  1. Upgrade LinkedIn to Sales Navigator Advanced (5 min, £80/mo on your account).
  2. Spend 10 minutes scanning the long list with me and approving / killing duds.
  3. Send the messages manually through LinkedIn — protects your account, ~30 minutes for 50 messages.
  4. Update Attio as people reply (drag-and-drop kanban — takes seconds).
