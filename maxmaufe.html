The build

Four components. One funnel.

This is what I’d build for you. We can ship them in this order — get the first one working, bolt on the next.

01

Long-list builder

I take a JD (or just your search terms) and the system runs a deep search across LinkedIn, public data and a few signals you can’t see manually. Returns 50-100 candidates with role, location, current company, and a flag for ‘might be open to move.’ The bit that currently eats 3-4 days of your week.

02

Personalised outreach

For each candidate, the system drafts a first LinkedIn message in your voice — not generic, references something specific from their profile. You skim, send, or edit before sending. Ten messages instead of typing the same one fifty times.

03

Reply tracking + follow-up nudges

The system watches who replies, moves them down the funnel, and nudges you to follow up with the ones who haven’t. No more ‘did I chase that guy?’ sitting in the back of your head.

04

Granola for the calls

Free tool, set up in 5 minutes. Sits in the background of your Zoom calls, records, gives you a clean summary and a follow-up email draft after every call. You stop forgetting which Swedish CTO said what.
