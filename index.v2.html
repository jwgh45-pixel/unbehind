How it works

Four steps. No SOWs, no decks.

From first call to ongoing work. Most clients have something shipped within a fortnight.

Step 01 · Day zero

30-minute call

You tell me what’s on fire. I tell you whether I’m the right person for it. If we’re both in, we pick a package and a start date. No discovery decks. No SOWs.

Step 02 · Week 1

One day, one priority

I block the first day of the month. We agree the priority for that day at 9am. By 6pm there’s an artefact in your hands — a working GPT, a sales pipeline, a brand system, a dashboard. Not advice on how to build one.

Step 03 · Async between

Slack, not status reports

Quick questions get quick answers. Bigger asks queue for the next day. No retainers-creep. No agency-style timesheets. You see what I’m doing in real time.

Step 04 · Rolling month-on-month

Cancel any time

Monthly billing. No notice required. If we’re not adding value, you stop. If we are, we keep going. Most engagements run 6–12 months until you hire an FTE or wind down a project.

What you’re probably thinking

The honest answers.

What’s Claude Code, and why does it matter?

Claude Code is Anthropic’s terminal-based coding agent — the most powerful tool a non-engineer can use to ship working software in 2026. ChatGPT chats. Claude Code builds. Every project you see in the strip above was built solo with it, by a non-technical operator. That’s the whole differentiator.

You said you have a day job. Is this sustainable?

Yes. The cap of 3 clients exists for exactly this reason. Two days a month per client × three clients = six days a month outside of Regeno — doable, and capped on purpose. If we’re full, you go on the waitlist.

What if I want a specialist, not a generalist?

Then this isn’t for you, and that’s fine. Specialists do one thing brilliantly. I do many things well, fast, with AI — better suited to founder-led businesses where the priority changes month-to-month. If you know exactly what you need built and it never changes, hire a specialist.

Who’s the work for, exactly?

Founders running £500k–£5m businesses without a senior commercial / ops / brand hire. Heritage and lifestyle brands. Boutique studios. Single-founder consultancies. Premium wellness practices. People who’ve outgrown “founder does everything” but can’t justify a £100k FTE.

Confidentiality, contracts, paperwork?

Standard mutual NDA on engagement. Rolling monthly contract, cancel any time. Invoiced through Unbehind Ltd to your company. Anything I build for you is yours — prompts, GPTs, code, copy — you keep it after we wrap.

Who is this not for?

Big companies (you need a real team). VC-backed startups with engineering leads (you have one already). Anyone shopping for advice or strategy decks. Anyone who wants to learn AI themselves — that’s a course, this is a service.
